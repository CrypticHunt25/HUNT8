Step 4: The Triwizard Tournament Challenge

The champions competed fiercely, but only true logic can uncover their final scores.

"At the Triwizard Tournament, three champions—Harry, Fleur, and Viktor—competed in a series of tasks:
  • The first task gave Harry twice as many points as Viktor.
  • Fleur scored 10 fewer points than Viktor.
  • The total score for all three was 210.
Question 1: How many points did each champion score?
Question 2: Multiply Harry’s score by 2 and subtract 15."
